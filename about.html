About Us

Welcome to BP, your number one source for all things hair and skincare. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2004 by Brian Porbeni, BP Solutions has come a long way from its beginnings in Milwaukee WI. When Brian first started out, their passion for Device sales, repairs and maintenance drove them to start the business and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the sales & service industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
